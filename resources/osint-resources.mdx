## Understanding phone numbers

Reference material for phone number formats, carrier conventions, and international dialing standards.

| Resource | Description |
|---|---|
| [whitepages.fr/phonesystem](http://whitepages.fr/phonesystem/) | Overview of the international phone numbering system |
| [Formatting International Phone Numbers](https://support.twilio.com/hc/en-us/articles/223183008-Formatting-International-Phone-Numbers) | Twilio guide to E.164 and international formatting |
| [National conventions for writing telephone numbers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_conventions_for_writing_telephone_numbers) | Wikipedia reference for country-specific formatting rules |
